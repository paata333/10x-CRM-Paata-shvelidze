# Glossary — 10X CRM

A curated list of terms used throughout 10X CRM and modern sales management.

---

## A

**Active Deal**
A client relationship currently in progress, with status other than "Won" or "Lost". Represents ongoing sales opportunities.

**Avatar**
A small profile image or icon representing a user or client. In 10X CRM, avatars display client initials if no image is available.

---

## C

**Client**
A prospect, customer, or organization tracked in 10X CRM. Each client has contact information, deal value, status, and notes.

**CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**
Software designed to help sales teams manage client interactions, track pipeline, and close deals efficiently.

**Current Password**
The user's existing password required to authenticate sensitive account changes, such as password updates.

---

## D

**Dark Theme**
A display mode using dark backgrounds with light text, designed to reduce eye strain in low-light environments.

**Deal Value**
The monetary amount or potential revenue associated with a client. Used to calculate pipeline health and forecast revenue.

**Dashboard**
The main analytics and overview page showing key performance metrics: total clients, active deals, won revenue, and new contacts.

---

## F

**Filter**
A search refinement tool that narrows visible clients by a specific attribute (e.g., status: Lead, Contacted, Won, Lost).

---

## L

**Lead**
A potential client in the early stage of the sales pipeline. A "Lead" status indicates first contact or initial interest.

**localStorage**
Browser-based data storage that persists across page refreshes and browser sessions without requiring a server.

---

## N

**Notes**
Timestamped comments or follow-up reminders attached to a client record, useful for tracking conversations and next steps.

---

## P

**Pipeline**
The sales process flow, divided into stages (Lead → Contacted → Won/Lost). Visualized in the Dashboard as a pipeline overview.

---

## R

**Reminder**
A scheduled notification prompting the user to follow up with a specific client. In 10X CRM, reminders can be set for 1 minute intervals.

---

## S

**Session**
The authenticated state of a logged-in user, stored in localStorage and verified on every protected page load.

**Sort**
A data organization feature allowing users to reorder clients by newest, name (A→Z), or deal value (high→low).

**Status**
The current stage of a client relationship: Lead (new prospect), Contacted (initial outreach), Won (closed deal), or Lost (no longer pursuing).

---

## T

**Toast Notification**
A brief, auto-dismissing message (typically 3 seconds) providing user feedback on successful actions (green), errors (red), or info (blue).

**Theme Toggle**
A user preference control switching between light and dark display modes, stored persistently in localStorage.

---

## U

**User**
A person registered in 10X CRM with an email, password, and full name. Each user maintains their own client database and session.

---

## V

**Validation**
The process of checking user input (form fields) against business rules before submission (e.g., email format, password strength).

---

## W

**Won Revenue**
The total deal value of all clients with "Won" status, indicating closed deals and confirmed revenue.
